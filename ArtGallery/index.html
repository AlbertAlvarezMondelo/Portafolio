About Us

Welcome to ArtGallery, your source for exploring a world of art. We are a passionate team of art enthusiasts dedicated to bringing you a curated collection of art masterpieces.

Our goal is to provide you with a seamless and immersive experience of exploring art from different eras and cultures. We believe in the power of art to inspire and enrich our lives.

ArtGallery is proud to be powered with The Met API , which allows us to showcase a diverse range of artworks from The Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. Through this collaboration, we bring the treasures of The Met's collection to your fingertips.

Join us on this journey of artistic discovery, and let the beauty of art inspire you.

Albert Álvarez Mondelo - 2023
